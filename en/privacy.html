Privacy Policy

Personal Information Protection Policy

RISEby inc. ("we," "us," or "the Company") recognizes the protection of personal information as an important responsibility. We are committed to handling and protecting personal information appropriately in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

1. Definition of Personal Information

In this Policy, "personal information" refers to information about a living individual that can identify a specific individual through descriptions such as name, date of birth, or other information contained therein (including information that can be easily combined with other information to identify a specific individual).

2. Collection of Personal Information

We may collect personal information in the following circumstances:

  • When you contact us through our inquiry form
  • When you apply for our services
  • When you request materials or information
  • When you register for seminars or events

3. Purpose of Use

We use collected personal information for the following purposes:

  • Responding to and addressing inquiries
  • Providing and improving our services
  • Sending notifications and information from our company
  • Contract fulfillment and related administrative procedures
  • Marketing research and analysis

4. Disclosure to Third Parties

We will not provide personal information to third parties without your consent, except in the following cases:

  • When required by law
  • When necessary to protect life, body, or property
  • When particularly necessary for public health improvement or child welfare
  • When cooperating with government agencies or local authorities in performing duties prescribed by law

5. Security Management

We implement appropriate security measures to prevent leakage, loss, or damage to personal information. We also provide appropriate supervision to employees who handle personal information.

6. Disclosure, Correction, and Deletion

You may request disclosure, correction, addition, deletion, or suspension of use of your personal information. Upon such request, we will respond within a reasonable period after verifying your identity.

7. Use of Cookies

Our website uses cookies to improve services and analyze access patterns. Cookies do not collect personally identifiable information. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but some services may not function properly.

8. Analytics Tools

Our website uses Google Analytics, an analytics tool provided by Google Inc. Google Analytics uses cookies to collect traffic data. This traffic data is collected anonymously and does not identify individuals.

9. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may change this Policy due to changes in laws or business operations. The revised Policy shall take effect upon posting on our website.
